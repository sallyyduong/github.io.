Donation Drive

Role Director of Marketing
Organization Warren College Student Council
Time Spring 2018

I managed and planned a college-wide donation drive that helped collect clothing and household goods. The donations were used to raise money for both the International Center and for Disabled Veterans.

Over 150 goods were collected during the donation drive which was held at the end of the school year. The drive benefitted a local causes and diverted student waste.
